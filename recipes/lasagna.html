Traditional Lasagna

Lasagna is a traditional italian dish that is fufilling to make and even more satisfying to eat. With a little time and precision makes lasagna can be a piece of cake!

Ingredients

  1. Lasagna sheets
  2. Bolognese Sauce
    1. 1 can of San Marzano Tomatos
    2. Ground meat (Either turkey, beef or italian sausage
    3. Basil
    4. Garlic
    5. Salt and pepper
    6. 1 medium onion
  3. Ricotta cheese
  4. Parmesan

Recipe

  1. Prepare pasta sheets
    1. Bring water to boil and add two tablespoons of salt for flavor
    2. Once at rolling boil add pasta sheets,
      cook until al dente
    3. Drain water and put sheets to the side
  2. Prepare Bolognese sauce
    1. Add can of tomatos to a sauce pan and put at medium heat
    2. Add a tablespoon of olive oil and salt and pepper
    3. dice 1 onion
    4. Put into pan medium heat with olive oil
    5. Once onion is browned,add ground meat
    6. add mince garlic and continually stir until incorporated
    7. add diced basil leaves
    8. once everything is slightly browned add to sauce
    9. cook sauce thoroughly
  3. heat oven to 375
  4. Grease Lasagna pan
  5. Layering of lasagna
    1. Add layer bolognese sauce
    2. Add layer lasagna sheets
    3. add layer ricotta cheese
    4. Repeat steps 1-3 until pan is full or out of ingredients
  6. top with gratted parmesan cheese
  7. Put into oven for 35 mins and or until heated thoroughly
  8. Enjoy!
