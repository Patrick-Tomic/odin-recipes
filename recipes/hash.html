Hash breakfast!

Description

This easy to make breakfast is a great way to get your day started
with all of your needed nutrients to get you going!

Ingredients

  1. One Potato
  2. 3 Eggs
  3. Bacon
  4. Spinach
  5. One Onion

Recipe

  1. cut potato into 1/4 inch cubes
  2. Dice onion
  3. dice spinach leaves
  4. put cut ingredients in a bowl and to the side
  5. heat pan to medium and put three slices of bacon of choice in
    cook until crispy
  6. Take bacon out, if using pork bacon instantly put cut ingredients into pan,
    if not put a tablespoon of oil in and then ingredients
  7. cut up bacon and put into pan as well
  8. once potatos are crisp and golden add salt and pepper
  9. add eggs and stir!
  10. once cooked through take off heat and add seasoning to your liking
  11. enjoy!
