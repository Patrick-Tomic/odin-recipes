Grilled Cheese

Description

Good snack for a quick lunch!

Ingredients

  1. Two slices of bread of choice
  2. three slices of cheese of your choice

Recipe

  1. assemble cheese sandwich
  2. Heat pan to medium and add butter
  3. Once butter melted, add sandwich, and put on lid. Wait 4 mins
  4. flip sandwich and put lid back on, wait two mins
  5. Remove from heat and enjoy!
